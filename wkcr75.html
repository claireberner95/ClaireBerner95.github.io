WKCR 75th Anniversary, 2016

I spearheaded a rebranding project for WKCR-FM New York’s 75th anniversary that included a new logo and merchandise, and I worked with web designers to revamp the station’s website. The station sold the t-shirts as part of a successful fundraiser and still uses the logo on their Facebook page.

The main circular logo draws inspiration from 78 RPM records, referencing WKCR’s rich history and its celebration of that era of music. The station houses a vast library of vinyl (and shellac) records, and many of the shows run completely analog. A yellowed label reminiscent of early jazz records captures the station’s nostalgia, especially on their 75th anniversary.

Potential logo thumbnails drafted in 2015 for review by the then-station manager. After initial sketches, she and I decided on either a headphones or record motif, both important symbols to the station.
